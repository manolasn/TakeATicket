Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how the app developed by Nikiforos Manolas handles the collection, use, and protection of user data. This app integrates data from The Movie Database (TMDb).

1. Data Collection

The app does not collect or store any personal data. Any data displayed (such as movie titles, descriptions, or images) is fetched in real-time from TMDb.

2. Use of TMDb Data

All data provided by TMDb is used solely for display purposes. We do not modify or manipulate the data in any way, nor do we collect any user-generated content.

3. External Links

This app may contain links to external websites, including TMDb. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

4. Security

We take security seriously and implement measures to protect the data fetched from TMDb. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security over the network.

5. Changes to the Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the app following any changes will constitute your acknowledgment and acceptance of the updated policy.
